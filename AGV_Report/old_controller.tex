\section{Previous Controller Iterations}
\label{sec:old-controller}

The current controller was preceded by earlier implementations that helped establish the project requirements and expose practical limitations. This section records their main design choices so that the evolution of the system remains traceable.

\subsection{Initial Approach}

Describe the responsibilities and operating assumptions of the first controller. Focus on the motivation for the approach, the parts that were effective and the conditions under which it no longer scaled to the warehouse scenario.

\subsection{Lessons Learned}

Compare the earlier approach with the current controller in terms of robustness, maintainability and behaviour around dynamic obstacles. Present limitations as engineering observations rather than as unsupported claims, and explain how they informed subsequent design decisions.
